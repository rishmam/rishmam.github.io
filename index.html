FAQ

What is BearPairs?

BearPairs is a website with a goal - match Berkeley students for Valentine's Day! No one should have to be alone on February 14th, so we've made it our goal to match students based on attributes, personality, and other quirks :) We hope that you can find someone special!

How can I sign up?

It's super easy! Just create an account, fill out the survey, and wait for your profiles. You'll be able to see your matches and their profiles, and then you can go ahead and set up a date!

When will I get my matches?

Nice job filling out the survey and getting set up! You'll get your matches by February 13th, and then you can set up that perfect Valentine's day date!
